e-editiones is a Swiss non-profit society with cantonal tax-exempt status. Donations are tax-deductible in Switzerland to the extent permitted by your canton.

Your contribution funds the long-term maintenance of open source tools for digital scholarly editions — [TEI Publisher](https://tei-publisher.org/), [Roaster](https://github.com/eeditiones/roaster) and related infrastructure — community workshops, and the documentation that helps new editions get started.

Recurring institutional or individual support is just as welcome. See [how to become a member](/pages/how-to-become-a-member/) — institutional membership from CHF 500.– per year, individual from CHF 50.–.
