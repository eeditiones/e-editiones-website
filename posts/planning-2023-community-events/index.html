Community Meetings in 2023

Lars Windauer
06 Dec 2022

The community meetings are held online using this link, usually on the first Tuesday of each month at 17:00 CET/CEST. No registration is required to attend. We warmly invite you to propose topics info@e-editiones.org for the forthcoming meetings.

Dear community, the e-editiones board is currently planning 2023 and our community meetings. We want to revive the original idea of workshop reports and invite you to discuss open problems from your projects with us. It is a good opportunity to tap into concentrated expertise, which is why such a discussion is worthwhile even or especially at the beginning of projects. We still try to organise a meeting every first Tuesday of the month. So please get in touch if you want to present something.
