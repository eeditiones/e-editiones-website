Community meetup on [March 10, 2026 at 17:00 CET](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?msg=e-editiones+community+event&iso=20260310T17&p1=268&ah=1).


After the strong interest in our recent overview of the TEI-Publisher 10 release, the e-editiones team is happy to invite you to a follow-up community session designed to support users through the next step: upgrading from older versions to TEI-Publisher 10.

In the first event we introduced the new features, enhancements, and overall vision behind version 10. If you haven’t seen that yet, take a look here: 

* [TEI Publisher v10 celebration](https://youtu.be/Sa8edUuoZes)

Now it’s time to talk about practical implementation. During this community event we’ll walk through:

* **Why and when to update** - We’ll talk about the key benefits of moving to version 10 and how to assess whether your current installation is ready for an upgrade.
* **Upgrade process** - You’ll get a guide through the workflow from a legacy version to TEI-Publisher 10 — with explanations of what changes to expect and how to manage them.
* **Caveats and pitfalls** - Updating core tooling can be tricky. We’ll highlight specific areas where issues commonly arise so you can plan ahead and avoid surprises.
* **Advanced topics** (time permitting) - If the session allows, we’ll touch on more advanced workflows such as templating in TEI-Publisher 10 — and how the new version changes or improves existing approaches.

Who Should Attend? This session is for:

* Developers and digital editors currently using TEI-Publisher
* Teams planning to migrate to version 10
* Anyone who wants hands-on insight into the update to TEI-Publisher 10 process
* Community members interested in deeper workflows and best practices

Join Us! Bring your questions — and your upgrade plans! We look forward to a lively, practical discussion.
