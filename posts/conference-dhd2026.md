e-editiones will be participating in the [DHd2026](https://dhd2026.digitalhumanities.de/?page_id=21&lang=en) in Vienna from February 23–27, 2026.


On Tuesday, February 24, e-editiones together with the Universitätsbibliothek Kiel will offer a full-day on-site workshop:

**TEI Publisher reloaded: Digitale Editionen mit System – modular, nachhaltig, community-orientiert**

The workshop will focus on structured, sustainable, and community-oriented approaches to building digital editions with TEI Publisher.


Workshop information and materials are available at: [e-editiones.org/dhd2026](/dhd2026)

Please note: the workshop and all resources are in German.

We are very much looking forward to meeting colleagues and friends in Vienna!
