Community meetup on [April 07, 2026 at 17:00 CEST](https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?msg=e-editiones+community+event&iso=20260407T17&p1=268&ah=1).

**[Slides](https://github.com/eeditiones/workshop/blob/master/slides/e-editiones20260407.pdf) from the meetup are now available on GitHub**.

This meetup is meant as a follow up on the [February talk about TEI Publisher 10](/posts/community-event-tei-publisher-10/). While we covered the basics in the previous session, this meetup will concentrate more on best practice questions.

* how to recreate an existing TEI Publisher app with TP 10
* best practices to ensure smooth future updates
* development workflow
  * when to regenerate the application via jinks?
  * how to sync local changes to the database?
  * how to sync database changes to local directory and git?
* caveats and traps to watch out for

We'll have a few developers in the room who already implemented several new TEI Publisher 10 projects and ported older applications to it. This will give us an oportunity to discuss things from a very practical perspective.

We meet Tuesday April 7 at 17:00 CEST in [Teams](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/meetup-join/19%3ameeting_MGFmZTUzMDMtYzg4OS00YTA3LTg3YjctYTY1ZWM5OWI2MTg4%40thread.v2/0?context=%7b%22Tid%22%3a%22bd21f2e9-9af5-42a1-8caf-eb264278467f%22%2c%22Oid%22%3a%227e4db3d1-dfdc-46a5-b4f2-aaea8a156edb%22%7d)!
