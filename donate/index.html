Support e-editiones

e-editiones is a Swiss non-profit society with cantonal tax-exempt status. Donations are tax-deductible in Switzerland to the extent permitted by your canton.

Your contribution funds the long-term maintenance of open source tools for digital scholarly editions — TEI Publisher, Roaster and related infrastructure — community workshops, and the documentation that helps new editions get started.
